## Introduction

The Multi-Source Data Analytics and Triangulation (MSDAT) platform is clearly focused on analytical presentation of key health indicators from multiple health data sources using interactive and intuitive visualizations.

The MSDAT version 2.0 is an optimized platform with improved product increment on functionalities and user friendliness. A one-stop analytics dashboard for performing various integrated analyses, viewing and comparing key health indicators from different data sources. Built to have seamless and easy access to routine, estimate and survey data, showing at a glance data sources and health indicators.

The platform was developed by key stakeholders to aid and improve decision making processes. It will also enable health practitioners to evaluate performance and address areas with pressing needs on the premise of qualitative analysis on health data.

## Problem Statement

Due to the relatively limited accessibility, and poor visualization practise of health data to stakeholders, policy makers and health practitioners to facilitate healthcare decisions. It is imperative to have a platform driven largely to provide valuable visual insights, analytics capabilities and interactive experience on data to both the executive health sphere and the clinical arsenal for decision making processes.

Hence , the crucial need to design and develop an optimized health data dashboard that enables healthcare professionals to access data based on integrity, accuracy and consistency in order to efficiently facilitate quality health care delivery.

## Objectives

1.) To provide a visual interface that eliminates delays in making key decisions by top stakeholders and medical practitioners.

2.) To provide quick and easy access to health data for analysis and interpretation

3.) To make accessible comparison of key health indicators across data sources.

4.) To present key health trends via graphs and charts as a premise to gain insights and make quick decisions.

## Goals

The aim of this improved platform is to ensure that decision-makers have the accessibility to health data and means to make informed decisions and policies to improve healthcare.

A visualization interface where key stakeholders and policy makers can track and monitor health data across different sources within the health precinct.
